Riverview

Riverview is a peaceful destination located along Asin Road near Baguio City. It offers a relaxing environment where visitors can enjoy scenic river views, fresh mountain air, and the beauty of nature.

Key Features:

  • Beautiful river scenery
  • Fresh and cool mountain air
  • Perfect spot for picnics and relaxation
  • Great for photography and sightseeing
